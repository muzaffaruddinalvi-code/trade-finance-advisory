Trust & Compliance

This platform is operated by an independent trade finance consultant supporting client introductions and transaction origination in collaboration with regulated banking partners.

Key Positioning:
Independent consultant role focused on introductions, structuring discussions, and deal origination support.

Scope of Services

  • Facilitating introductions to authorized banking teams
  • Supporting initial discussions on trade finance structures
  • Assisting in aligning transaction requirements before formal bank engagement

Important Limitations

  • No financial instruments are issued
  • No funds are accepted, handled, or transferred
  • No financial, investment, or legal advice is provided
  • No binding representation of any bank or financial institution

Bank-Led Process

All trade finance transactions are subject to the internal policies and procedures of the respective bank, including:

  • Know Your Customer (KYC) verification
  • Anti-Money Laundering (AML) compliance checks
  • Sanctions screening
  • Credit and risk assessment
  • Final approval by the issuing institution

Client Expectations

  • Provide accurate and verifiable business information
  • Engage in legitimate commercial transactions
  • Comply with all applicable regulations
  • Understand that all instruments are subject to approval

Confidentiality & Professional Conduct

All discussions are treated with strict confidentiality and handled in accordance with professional standards, including data protection and compliance considerations.

Disclaimer
